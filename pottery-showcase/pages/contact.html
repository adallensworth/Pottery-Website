Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept commissions?

Yes, I welcome commission requests for custom pottery pieces. Please contact me with details about what you're looking for, and we can discuss the possibilities, timeline, and pricing.

How do I care for my pottery?

Most of my functional pieces are dishwasher and microwave safe, but I recommend hand washing for longevity. Avoid sudden temperature changes which can cause cracking. Detailed care instructions are included with each purchase.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Each piece is carefully packaged to ensure safe arrival.

Do you offer pottery classes or workshops?

Yes, I regularly host workshops for beginners and intermediate potters. Check the blog section for upcoming workshop dates or contact me for private lessons.
